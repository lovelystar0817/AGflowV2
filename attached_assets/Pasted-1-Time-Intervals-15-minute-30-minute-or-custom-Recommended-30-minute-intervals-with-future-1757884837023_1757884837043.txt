1. Time Intervals: 15-minute, 30-minute, or custom?

✅ Recommended: 30-minute intervals, with future option to customize.

Why:

30-min is the sweet spot for most salon services

Keeps UI clean (e.g., 10:00, 10:30, 11:00…)

Most booking systems (Square, Vagaro) default to 30 min

Easier to build now, can add advanced options later

Optional later: Let stylists define default interval in their profile (15/30/60)

✅ 2. Business Hours Constraint: Should availability be limited to them?

✅ Recommended: Yes, but soft constraint, not enforced.

What to do:

Let stylists set their business hours in /profile-setup

Show a subtle indicator (e.g. ⚠️ “Outside your usual hours”) if they pick times outside those bounds

Don’t prevent it — stylists are independent and may have exceptions

Why:

Gives guidance without friction

Keeps flexibility for emergencies or one-off late appointments

✅ 3. Default Weekly Availability for New Stylists?

✅ Yes, but only as a suggestion — not auto-applied.

What to do:

On first calendar visit, if stylist has no availability set:

Offer to “Use a default weekly schedule?”

Tues–Sat, 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

If they accept → create entries for the next 7 days

If not → leave it empty and let them fill manually

Why:

Reduces friction

Avoids confusion if a stylist forgets to set availability

Avoids accidental bookings when they're not ready

✅ 4. Appointment Integration — How Will This Work Later?

This is the big one. You need to design availability in a way that bookings can slot into it cleanly.

✅ Recommendation: Store availability as open windows (start + end)
Then later, use service duration + buffer to slice it into actual slots.

Future booking logic will look like:
- Availability: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM
- Client wants a 60-min haircut
- Buffer: 15 mins
→ Bookable slots: 10:00, 11:15, 12:30


So:

DO NOT store every possible slot

DO store flexible ranges

DO allow overlapping bookings later if needed (for stylists with assistants)

✅ TL;DR — Final Decisions
Question	Answer
Time Intervals	30-min default (configurable later)
Business Hour Constraints	Soft warning only
Default Weekly Schedule	Offer suggestion on first visit
Appointment Integration	Store time ranges, not individual slots — use services to generate slots later
